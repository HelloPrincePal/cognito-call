Microphone Access Required

Cognito Call needs your microphone to record your side of the conversation. Without it, only the other participants' audio will be captured.

  • Audio stays 100% local — never uploaded
  • Only active during recording
  • You can revoke access anytime in Chrome settings

⚠️ Important: When Chrome asks, choose "Allow" — not "Only this time". Temporary permissions don't work for background recording.

This page will close automatically after access is granted. You can then start recording from the extension popup.
